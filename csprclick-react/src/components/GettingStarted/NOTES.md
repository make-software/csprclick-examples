### Wallet Aggregator

CSPR.click’s Web SDK unifies access to every major wallet in the Casper ecosystem through a single API. It simplifies wallet integration for developers, removing the complexity of maintaining multiple SDKs and custom integrations.

Your dApp becomes instantly accessible to every user in the Casper network. This not only reduces development and maintenance costs but also minimizes user drop-off, driving higher onboarding success and long-term retention.

### Simplified Onboarding With Social Logins

CSPR.click enables seamless Single Sign-On (SSO) through popular social platforms like Google and Apple, allowing users to access your dApp instantly — no browser extensions or wallet setups required. It also supports creating self-custodial wallets linked directly to social accounts, combining convenience with security.

This streamlined onboarding process removes barriers for new users and provides a familiar, trustworthy experience for non-crypto-native audiences. The result is faster adoption, higher conversion rates, and a more inclusive entry point into the Casper ecosystem.

### Fiat On-Ramp

CSPR.click integrates multiple fiat on-ramp providers, enabling users to buy CSPR instantly using cards or wire transfers. Integration is fast, secure, and requires no extra setup on your side.

With access to providers across regions, users from multiple geographical areas can easily transact with your application, ensuring a truly global reach and smoother user experience.

### CSPR.cloud Proxy

CSPR.click includes a built-in client for CSPR.cloud, allowing your frontend application to query indexed data, listen to contract events, and interact directly with the Casper network RPC API — no backend required.

This approach simplifies development, lowers infrastructure costs, and speeds up your time to market while keeping your dApp fully connected to the Casper ecosystem.
